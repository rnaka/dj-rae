Crave

About Crave

This app will let users easily create a wishlist as they browse the internet! Add items to the list by inputing the name of the product, website title, and the description of the product if desired. View your wishlist so you can see any items you've seen in the past! There's no limit as to what you can add to your list!

How To Use The Crave App

Then go to any website and find an item you are interested in

Click on the 'create a wishlist/add item' button and Crave will detect the item to add to the wishlist

You can then view and edit your wishlist
