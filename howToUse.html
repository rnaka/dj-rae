Crave

About Crave

This app will let users easily create a wishlist as they browse the internet. Add items to the list with a simple push of a button. View your wishlist so you can see any items you've seen in the past! There's no limit as to what you can do with your list!

How To Use The Crave App

First, create your wishlist by clicking on 'Create a wishlist/add item' button and entering a name for your wishlist

Then go to any website and find an item you are interested in

Click on the 'create a wishlist/add item' button and Crave will detect the item to add to the wishlist

View your wishlist by clicking the "View your wishlist" button

From your wishlist, you can sort your wishlist, email your list, view the item, and delete items from your wishlist.

For more detailed information, visit our wikipage at https://github.com/rnaka/dj-rae/wiki.
